Conversation Pricing

WhatsApp Message Pricing Made Simple

Let's make WhatsApp API Pricing easy to understand for you!

Customer Service Window

When a user sends you a message, a 24-hour support window begins. Each new message from the user resets this window. During this window, you can respond freely without additional charges and respond with any free form messages. Also, utility templates delivered within this window won't be charged.

Unlimited Free Service Messages

Each business using Whatapi receives Unlimited FREE service messages. Businesses can reply to user messages without incurring any charges; it's completely FREE.

Marketing Messages

Marketing messages include all promotional messages, offers, and product updates. Each marketing message delivered costs ₹1.09/message (for Indian users)

Utility Messages

Utility messages are transactional in nature and include messages for delivery updates, transaction receipts, reminders and more. Each utility message delivered costs ₹0.145/message (for Indian users). Utility templates delivered within a customer service window won't be charged.

Authentication Messages

Authentication messages include messages used for verification purposes such as OTP, account registration and account recovery. Each authentication template message delivered costs ₹0.145/message (for Indian users)

Service Messages

Service messages includes all user-initiated messages related to customer support, Chatbot support & queries asked by users. Service conversations are FREE for all businesses using Whatapi.

Service messages continue to remain free within the 24-hour customer service window. These changes are automatically reflected on the Whatapi platform.

WhatsApp has shifted from per-conversation to per-template message pricing. Now, you'll pay only for each template message sent — giving you clearer control over messaging costs. You only pay for what you send, not for the entire conversation window.

A messaging session window starts when a user sends your WhatsApp Business Number a message and lasts for 24 hours from the most recently received message.
